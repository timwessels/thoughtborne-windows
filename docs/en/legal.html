Privacy policy

The short version: this site sets no cookies, runs no tracking, and embeds no external resources. There are no accounts, no forms, no newsletter — you cannot even enter data here. The only personal data that occurs at all is the hosting provider's technical access log, which is deleted after seven days at the latest.

Controller

Controller within the meaning of the GDPR is the person named in the imprint above. Privacy questions: t@wssls.net.

What happens when you visit this site

The site is static. When your browser requests a page, the hosting infrastructure processes the technically necessary access data — your IP address, the requested URL, date and time, and browser information. That is what any web server sees when it serves a page; nothing is collected on top of it.

Hosting

This site is hosted by ALL-INKL.COM – Neue Medien Münnich (Friedersdorf, Germany); its data centers are located in Dresden, Germany. When your browser requests a page, the server records the access — including your IP address — in a log file used to keep the service running securely and reliably. Log files are deleted after seven days at the latest. All data stays on servers in Germany; there is no transfer to countries outside the EU. A data processing agreement pursuant to Art. 28 GDPR is in place with the hosting provider.

The legal basis for this processing is Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR: the legitimate interest in delivering this site securely and reliably.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, and data portability, and the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority — for example the one of your place of residence. You are always welcome to write to t@wssls.net.

Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR)

You have the right to object at any time, on grounds relating to your particular situation, to processing of your personal data that is based on Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR. To object, email t@wssls.net.

What does not happen here

No cookies. No tracking or analytics. No external fonts, scripts, or embeds. No profiling, no automated decision-making. You are not required to provide any personal data to use this site. If any of this ever changes, this page changes first.
